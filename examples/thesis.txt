NVIDIA (NVDA) — long thesis.

NVIDIA is the defining platform of the AI era, and its dominance is durable with years
of runway ahead. Three things underpin the case:

1. Demand for its data-center AI accelerators structurally exceeds supply, and will keep
   doing so for the foreseeable future.
2. It faces no credible competition in AI training — the CUDA software moat locks in
   developers and customers worldwide.
3. Its global reach, including continued access to the large China market, supports
   sustained double-digit data-center revenue growth.

Because the growth runway is long and largely un-disrupted, the premium valuation is
justified.
